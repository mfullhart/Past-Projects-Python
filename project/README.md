# Banana Quality Prediction

## Problem Domain

I will be examining banana data to predict if a banana is considered good or bad based on a variety of characteristics. 
I chose this data set because I love banana bread and this seemed interesting.

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/l3llff/banana

## Data

Characteristics Available:

Size - size of fruit

Weight - weight of fruit

Sweetness - sweetness of fruit

Softness - softness of fruit

HarvestTime - amount of time passed from harvesting of the fruit

Ripeness - ripeness of fruit

Acidity - acidity of fruit

## Experimental Design

X = Characteristics listed above in data

Y = Quality (Good, Bad)

## Discussion

The listed characteristics are already numeric, so I will not need to do any one-hot encoding. 
The results will be classified into to two categories, good and bad, based on the combined scores of their characteristics.
