# Hypothesis Testing

## Hypothesis

I hypothesize that instant ramen in a bowl has a higher average rating out of 5 stars compared to instant ramen in a cup.

I think this is because of the material the bowl is made out of vs cups, which are made with styrofoam.

- A vs B comparison, with some specific measure stated
- A: Rating of bowl ramen
- B: Rating of cup ramen
- Comparison: A > B

Comparison could be any of the following:
A < B
A > B
A != B (not equal)
A == B (equal)

Ideally, each group (A,B):
- Has hundreds or thousands of rows in the data set
- Would have the same (or very similar) # of rows

Your hypothesis must be detailed and specific enough that it is testable and repeatable 
    by other scientists

## Dependent Variable

* Rating (Out of 5 starts)
* The dependent variable(thing) that depends on whether it is from group A or B 
  * Ex: Lunar or Martian

## Independent Variable(s)

* Ramen style: Bowl vs Cup
* The independent variable(thing) that does not depend on anything else, it just 'is'...
  * Dependent variable might vary depending on the independent variable
* Is the sample from group A or B? 
  * The group (A,B) is the independent variable

## Controlled Variable(s)

* Brand
* Data Range (2009-2024)

* Things that are controlled in the dataset itself, 
     or things you choose to constrain/restrictive on your side

## Uncontrolled Variable(s)

* Date produced
* Amount of ramen made per serving
* Cooking directions (microwave vs hot water)
* When and where was the ramen made?

* These are things that were not carefully controlled during data collection
* In this example, uncontrolled variables are things that might impact or change 
  the meteorite mass that are not the lunar vs Martian grouping

## Planned t-Test

* One-tailed or two-tailed: one-tailed
* Independent or paired samples: independent

* Notes:
  * t-test is a statistical test of significant differences in means of two groups

p-value, t-stat = t-test([rating of bowl ramen], [rating of cup ramen], ...)


## Results

* Bowls have a higher average rating than cups :)
